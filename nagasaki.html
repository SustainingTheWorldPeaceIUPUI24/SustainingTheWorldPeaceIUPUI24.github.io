Discovering Nagasaki's History: Day 5

We went to the Nagasaki Hypocenter Park and got a very informative tour, featuring many statues and a brief glimpse of the inside of the Uragami Cathedral. Afterwards we went to the memorial sight for Dr. Nagai Takeshi, a famous doctor who spent most of his time helping those effected by the atomic bomb.

Exploring the Peace Museum and memorial

Hibakusha's (A-Bomb Survivor) Testimony: Day 6

We went to the Hibakusha Community Center, and heard A-bomb survivor Ms. Shizuka Mitamura testimony in the style of a kamishibai story. It was very insightful and she was very polite. She even signed a couple of our students notebooks. Afterwards we had an art workshop with Mr. Shinichiro Matsuo. It was very fun and lot of our pieces were well liked by Mr. Shinichiro Matsuo and are going to be displayed back home at IUI.

Meeting with Hibakusha and creating art at The Hibakusha Community Center
